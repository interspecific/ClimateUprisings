Let's take control of the ship.

Communities around the world are advancing bold, effective, and equitable climate policies. It’s time we do the same. This site is your toolkit for action: full of data, strategies, model legislation, and organizing resources. Whether you're a student, staffer, policymaker, or concerned resident—this is where we move forward together.

Lobby for Change: Push Local and State Policy

Use this platform to advocate for city, county, and state-level action. Local change builds momentum nationally.

  • Meet with your city council or mayor to propose a climate action plan
  • Support zoning reform, transit expansion, and green jobs training programs
  • Use our Policy Guide to share proven frameworks and real-world examples
  • Back state-level efforts like the Polluters Pay Climate Superfund
  • Encourage your school board or institution to adopt climate curriculum and sustainability programs

Think Globally, Act Locally

The U.S. has both a responsibility and an opportunity. Pushing for bold change at home supports the global climate justice movement—led by scientists, frontline communities, Indigenous organizers, and youth across the world.

  • We align with the UNFCCC, the IPCC AR6, and the UNDP Climate Promise
  • We support net-zero transitions, just transition frameworks, and rights-based solutions
  • We advocate for U.S. investment in global adaptation, clean energy, and equitable development
